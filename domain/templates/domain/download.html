DIGGER Networks & databases


File name Description Link
Joint PPI and DDI network Joint network graph that integrates PPIs and DDIs and in which nodes represent protein domains defined by concatenating Entrez and Pfam id. The edges between the nodes represent DDIs which are defined if the domains are known to interact and if the respective proteins are also PPI partners (.GraphML). Download
Mapping of protein domains to exons Database table maps transcripts and exons to their isoforms and Pfam domains Download
Position-specific residue annotations of the PPI PPI network of the human proteome based on experimentally resolved structures in the Protein Data Bank (PDB). This database contains the position of the individual amino acid positions (~1.5 GB). Download
Position-specific residue PPI mapped to exons This database contains exons that encode for residue for every structurally resolved interactions. Download
