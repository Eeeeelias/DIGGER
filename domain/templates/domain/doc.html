DIGGER Documentation & Help



General workflow for AS analysis using DIGGER.

DIGGER integrates protein-protein interaction and domain-domain interactions into a joint graph and maps interacting residues to exons. DIGGER allows the users to query exons or isoforms individually or as a set to visually explore their interactions. The three modes of DIGGER can be used interchangeably.
