DIGGER


By mapping the domain-domain Interactions to the protein-protein interactions, DIGGER can provide a visualization of the interacted regions between the proteins. In addition, the tool can analyse the effect of the splicing variations in the Interactome, starting from a single alternative splicing event to a set of protein variants.
